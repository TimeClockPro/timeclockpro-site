Frequently Asked Questions

Is TimeClockPro free?

Yes! You can start with our free plan which includes basic time tracking for one job. Upgrade to Pro for advanced features like multiple jobs, overtime rules and reports.

When will TimeClockPro launch?

TimeClockPro is launching in Q1 2025 for iOS devices. Join our waitlist above to get notified when we're ready for download.

Which platforms will be supported?

We're launching on iOS first, with support for iPhone and iPad. A web dashboard and Android version are planned for future updates.

Do I need an account?

No account is required for personal use. All your data stays on your device. If you choose to sync your data across devices or upgrade to Business for team features, you can sign in for cloud backup.

How is overtime calculated?

Our engine supports daily, weekly and consecutive-day overtime rules. You can choose from common presets (like California or Federal rules) or customize your own thresholds and multipliers.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Pro users can export their time tracking data to CSV format for easy import into payroll systems or spreadsheets.
