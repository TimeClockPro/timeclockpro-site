Frequently Asked Questions

Is TimeClockPro free?

Yes! You can start with our free plan which includes basic time tracking for one job. Upgrade to Pro for advanced features like multiple jobs, overtime rules and reports.

Which platforms are supported?

TimeClockPro is currently available for iOS devices. A web dashboard is planned for future updates.

Do I need an account?

No account is required for personal use. If you choose to sync your data across devices or manage teams, you can sign in for cloud backup.

How is overtime calculated?

Our engine supports daily, weekly and consecutive-day overtime rules. You can choose from presets or customise your own thresholds and multipliers.
