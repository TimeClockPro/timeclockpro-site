TimeClockPro Support

We're here to help you get the most out of TimeClockPro.

Common Issues

  • Can’t clock in/out? Make sure you granted the app location and motion permissions.
  • Hours not syncing? Check that your internet connection is stable before submitting shifts.
  • Incorrect pay rates? Double‑check the job configuration and active pay rules.

Need Additional Help?

If your issue isn’t listed above, here’s how to get more assistance:

  • Create an issue: On our support repository, click “New issue” and describe your problem.
  • Email us: Send a message to worktimetrack@gmail.com and we’ll respond within one business day.
